Have you ever imagined fried potatoes without ketchup, pizzas without tomatoe sausce or chips with black points?????
...You don't have to imagine it because here in Bio Market is our responsibility to take care that above scenarios won't happen.

How all this started???

Bio Market has started as a small family business more 30 years ago, but the begginings were not so good because the market was not so open as today.
You may wonder how we went from a small business to a big one that even exports outside of the country.
This was able only by hard work from our side and trust from the local farmers and clients.

But what we offer here?

We collect fruits and vegetables from locals in good prices for both parts

We make analyses of the products in our high-tehnologies labs

If products are within standarts we store them in our warehouse

Than we take orders from our partners inside or outside the country

You could came to take the products here or we could deliver them to your location... but this will add some costs.

We are open from Monday to Friday, but during late spring and summer we work Monday to Saturday.
